About Us:

Uhambo Farms started out in 2021 on a farm in Potchefstroom; breeding and growing cattle, sheep, goats, chickens and pigs for the local meat trade. However, when transporting these animals we noticed immense interest from the local community in purchasing the animals for rituals and cultural ceremonies. After some investigation, we moved away from the abattoirs and worked directly with the end consumer. Thus filling a need, creating jobs and providing more affordable meat to local communities. This was the birth of Moses Livestock Market.

Services we offer:

We are a livestock and animal feed outlet; delivering high quality, healthy livestock and feed to areas that are in close proximity clients. We specialize in cattle, sheep, goat, pig and chicken but have access to duck, turkey, pigeon, rabbit, bush pig and many more upon request.
