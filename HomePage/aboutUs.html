About Us

"We strive to make buyers feel alive"

WE ARE STOKVEL

Uhambo Farms started out in 2021 on a farm in Potchefstroom; breeding and growing cattle, sheep, goats, chickens and pigs for the local meat trade. However, when transporting these animals we noticed immense interest from the local community in purchasing the animals for rituals and cultural ceremonies. After some investigation, we moved away from the abattoirs and worked directly with the end consumer. Thus filling a need, creating jobs and providing more affordable meat to local communities. This was the birth of Moses Livestock Market.

Our Mission

It is our mission to provide a large variety of healthy, quality and affordable livestock to the community, serving all members no matter their budget. We strive to eliminate many of the barriers that our clients are facing by providing professional slaughtering, cutting, packaging, and delivery services at affordable rates. All while empowering and strengthening the local community through job creation and its farmers by providing quality animal feeds that will take their business to the next level.

Our Vision

After conquering Soweto, we hope to move on to other big locations where we can conveniently and affordably offer our services to mitigate all the costs incurred with the distance they need to acquire our product. We plan to build a brand that will set the standard for live markets and prevent communities from being exploited.
