1. Introduction

Welcome to Stokvel. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website www.stokvel.com (the "Site") and participate in our live animal auctions.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you register on our Site, participate in auctions, or contact us, we may collect personal information that includes, but is not limited to:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address.
  • Account Information: Username, password, and auction-related preferences.
  • Payment Information: Credit card numbers, billing addresses, and other payment details.

2.2 Non-Personal Information

We may also collect non-personal information automatically as you interact with our Site, such as:

  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and interaction data.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: Information collected through cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Manage Our Services: Facilitate auction participation, process transactions, and manage your account.
  • To Communicate with You: Send you updates, newsletters, marketing information, and respond to inquiries.
  • To Improve Our Services: Analyze usage patterns, enhance website functionality, and develop new features.
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations: Adhere to legal requirements and protect our rights.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist with payment processing, hosting, analytics, and other operational needs.
  • For Legal Reasons: To comply with legal obligations, respond to legal processes, or enforce our policies.
  • In Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Choices

6.1 Access and Update

You may access, correct, or update your personal information by logging into your account or contacting us.

6.2 Opt-Out

You may opt out of receiving marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in the emails we send or by contacting us directly.

6.3 Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our Site.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such information, we will take steps to delete it.
