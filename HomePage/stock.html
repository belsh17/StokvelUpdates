    Our Pigs

    Weight Range: 60kg-120kgs
    Landrace, large white and f1s (duroc boar) readily available for both slaughter and breeding. Kolbroek pigs and bush pigs available on request.

    Our Cattle

    Weight Range: 60kg-120kgs
    Meat breeds such as bonsmara, brahman, beefmaster and Nguni ranging from A-grade to C-grade will be available in a variety of colors to meet every client’s needs. Transport, slaughter, cutting & packaging and cold storage options will be available

    Our Sheep

    Weight Range: 60kg-120kgs
    A variety of meat master, merino, dorper and dormer ranging from lamb to mutton will be available. Pregnant ewes and breeding rams available on request.

    Our Goats

    Weight Range: 60kg-120kgs
    Providing all 3 of the major meat breeds (Boerbok, Kalahari Red and Savannah) as well as indigenous goats for slaughter to accommodate all of our client’s colour requirements. Kapaters/whethers, rams and mature ewes. Breeding Boerbok ewes and rams available on request.

    Our Chickens
